About Me

I just completed Flatiron School’s data science program with the aim of working with data in the education and/or social justice arena. I am a former English teacher and cook and I want to bring some of my on the ground insight working in populations with large percentages of English Language Learners to find new ways of optimizing services and learning outcomes. I’m particularly interested in making data digestible to a wide audience in the same ways that a good teacher can break down a difficult concept. I’m currently working on developing a front end to a sentiment classifier that takes in parent and student comments from a discussion board and will give you back a score as to how a community feels about their school as well as a list of frequent topics.
